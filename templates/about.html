About GreenScan

GreenScan is an AI-powered, web-based plant disease detection platform designed to help farmers, gardeners, and agriculture enthusiasts quickly identify plant diseases using images. By simply uploading a photo of a plant leaf, users receive instant disease detection along with actionable treatment recommendations.

Who GreenScan Is For

GreenScan is built for a wide range of users, including small-scale and large-scale farmers, home gardeners, agriculture students, and researchers. It is especially valuable for users who lack access to agricultural experts but need reliable, easy-to-understand plant health insights.

Supported Plants & Diseases

GreenScan leverages a pre-trained deep learning model based on the PlantVillage dataset, which contains thousands of labeled images across multiple crops. The system currently supports diseases affecting plants such as Apple, Corn, Grape, Potato, Tomato, Pepper, Soybean, Strawberry, and more.

How GreenScan Works

Once an image is uploaded, GreenScan processes it using a convolutional neural network (CNN) to detect whether the plant is healthy or diseased. If a disease is identified, the system recommends region-specific treatments where available, or general treatment solutions as a fallback.

The Future of GreenScan

GreenScan is designed to grow. Future enhancements include expanding plant and disease coverage, improving treatment databases, and supporting additional regions. The long-term vision is to make GreenScan a reliable digital companion for sustainable farming and smarter plant care.
